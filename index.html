About Quardian

Quardian designs Quantum-Safe and Quantum-Optimized infrastructures that secure and accelerate AI, IoT, and distributed systems. We combine post-quantum cryptography (Kyber, Dilithium, Falcon) with quantum-inspired optimization (QAOA/VQC) to make data exchanges trustworthy and computation efficient — today.

Our developer-first SaaS provides secure APIs, optimization engines, observability dashboards, and a clean integration experience. Built for researchers, startups, and enterprises piloting quantum-era transformation.
